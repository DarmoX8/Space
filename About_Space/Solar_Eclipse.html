Solar Eclipse

During the motion of the Moon around the Earth, the Moon sometimes comes between the Sun and the Earth. The Moon blocks the sunlight and casts its shadow on the Earth. This phenomenon is called Solar Eclipse. A Solar Eclipse occurs at day.

Total Solar Eclipse

When the sunlight is completely blocked and the Sun is completely hidden by the Moon or when the moon casts a umbra in the area, it is called a Total Solar Eclipse.

Partial Solar Eclipse

When the sunlight is partially blocked by some part of the sun can be seen or when the moon casts a penumbra on the area, it is called a Partial Solar Eclipse.
