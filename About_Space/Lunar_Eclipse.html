Lunar Eclipse

A lunar eclipse occurs when the Earth comes between the Sun and the Moon in a straight line. The Earth blocks the sunlight and casts ts shadow on the Moon. This is called Lunar Eclipse. A Lunar Eclipse occurs at day.

Total Lunar Eclipse

When the sunlight is completely blocked by the Earth or when the Earth casts a umbra on the moon and the moon is completely hidden by the shadow of the Earth, it is called a Total Lunar Eclipse

Partial Lunar Eclipse

When the shadow of the Earth partially covers the Moon or when the Earth casts a penumbra on the moon and some part of the Moon can be seen from the Earth, it is called a Partial Lunar Eclipse
