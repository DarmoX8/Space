Saturn

Saturn
Saturn has been known since ancient times because it can be seen without advanced telescopes. Saturn's upper atmosphere is divided into bands of clouds.
6th Planet from the Sun.
Specialty: 2nd Largest planet in our solar system. It have thousands of beautiful ringlets.
Other Names: Shani
Rotation: A speed of 9.87 km/s (22078.56 mph) and a 10 hours 42 minutes for a complete rotation.
Revolution: A speed of 21,637 mph (34,821 kph) and a 29 years for a complete revolution.
Moon: 82 Moons
Axis Tilt: 26.73°
Temperature: -285°F (-176.111°C)
Gravity: 10.44 m/s2 or 106.4/100 of Earth
Discovered: 1610 by Galileo Galilei
Atmosphere: 75% hydrogen and 25% helium with traces of other substances like methane and water ice
Age: 4.503 billion years
Distance from the Sun: 1.4826 billion km
Radius: 58,232 km
Diameter: 116,460 km
Mass: 5.683 x 10^26 kg
Surface Area: 42.7 billion km2
Rings: 7 Rings
