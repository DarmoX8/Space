Mercury

Mercury
If you lived on Mercury, you'd have a birthday every three months! Your weight on Mercury would be 38% of your weight on Earth. A solar day on the surface of Mercury lasts 176 Earth days.
1st Planet from the Sun.
Specialty: 1st Planet in the Solar System. Fastest planet revolving around the Sun.
Other Names: Budh
Rotation: A speed of 29 mps (47 km/s) and 56.85 days for a complete rotation.
Revolution: A speed of 6.76797503 mph (10.892 km/h), and 88 days for a complete revolution.
Moon: No Moons
Axis Tilt: 2°
Temperature: 354°F (178.889°C)
Gravity: 3.7 m/s2 or 37.8/100 of Earth
Discovered: 265 BC by Timocharis
Atmosphere: 42% oxygen, 29% sodium, 22% hydrogen, 6% helium, 0.5% potassium
Age: 4.503 billion years
Distance from the Sun: 69.446 million km
Radius: 2,439.7 km
Diameter: 4,879.4 km
Mass: 3.285 x 10^23 kg
Surface Area: 74.8 million km2
