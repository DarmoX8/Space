Neptune

Neptune
Neptune is dark, cold, and very windy. It's more than 30 times as far from the sun as Earth is.
8th Planet from the Sun.
Specialty: The Last Planet in the Solar System.
Other Names: Varun
Rotation: A speed of 23.5 km/s (52568 mph) and a 16 hours 6 minutes for a complete rotation.
Revolution: A speed of 5.43 km/s (12,146 mph) and a 165 years for a complete revolution.
Moons: 14 Moons
Axis Tilt: 28.32°
Temperature: -331°F (-201°C)
Gravity: 11.15 m/s2 or 112.5/100 of Earth
Discovered: 23 September 1846 by Urbain Le Verrier
Atmosphere: 80% hydrogen, 19% helium and a trace amount of methane
Age: 4.503 billion years
Distance from the Sun: 4.4749 billion km
Radius: 24,622 km
Diameter: 49,244 km
Mass: 1.024 x 10^26 kg
Surface Area: 7.618 billion km2
Rings: 5 Rings and 4 Ring Arcs
