Stars

Stars is a huge ball of burning gases. It produces its own light. The Sun is also a star. The stars are much bigger and brighter than the Sun. Yet, they appear tiny and faint in comparison to the Sun. This is because the Sun is much closer to the other stars. On a clear night sky, we can find many groups of stars forming beautiful patterns in the sky. These groups of stars are called constellations. There are total 88 known constellations.
