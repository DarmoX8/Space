Pluto

Pluto
Pluto an area full of icy bodies and other dwarf planets at the edge of our solar system. Pluto is just one of many icy objects in a distant area of our solar system.
It is a Dwarf Planet from the Sun.
Rotation: 6.4 days for a complete rotation.
Revolution: A speed of 10,603 miles per hour (4.74 km/s) and a 247.92 years for a complete revolution.
Moons: 5 Moons
Axis Tilt: 57°
Temperature: -387°F (-232°C)
Gravity: 0.62 m/s2 or 6.7/100 of Earth
Discovered: 18 February 1930 by Clyde Tombaugh
Age: 4.6 billion years
Distance from the Sun: 5.9 billion kilometers (3.7 billion miles)
Radius: 1,188.3 km
Diameter: 2,376.6 km
Mass: 1.30900 x 10^22 kg
Surface Area: 16.7 million kilometers2
