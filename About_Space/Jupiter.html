Jupiter
Jupiter is similar to a star, but it never got massive enough to start burning. It is covered in swirling cloud stripes. It has big storms like the Great Red Spot, which has been going for hundreds of years.
5th Planet from the Sun.
Specialty: The Biggest Planet in the Solar System, has the highest amount of gravity and the fastest rotating planet.
Other Names: Brahaspati
Rotation: A speed of 28,000 mph (12.51712 km/s) and a 9 hours 56 minutes for a complete rotation.
Revolution: A speed of 29,236 mph (13.0696614 km/s) and a 12 years for a complete revolution.
Moons 79 Moons
Axis Tilt: 3.13°
Temperature: -238°F (-150°C)
Gravity: 24.79 m/s2 or 236.4/100 of Earth
Discovered: 1610 by Galileo Galilei
Atmosphere: 90% hydrogen, 10% helium and small traces of other gases
Age: 4.603 billion years
Distance from the Sun: 748.39 million km
Radius: 69,911 km
Diameter: 139,820 km
Mass: 1.898 x 10^27
Surface Area: 61.42 billion km2
